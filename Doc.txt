All features : 
	Click on a cell -> shows all possible actions : 
		 	   - Harvest (get the nearest available worker and move it to the cell)
		 	   - Guard (get the nearest available knight and moce it to the cell
		 	   - Drop (makes the clicked worker go to town to loot his stuff)
		 	   
	Continuously -> forest grows (only on the cell -> has more wood)
	
	Resources -> Each cell has a precise amount of resources (specified only by the cell's Id)
